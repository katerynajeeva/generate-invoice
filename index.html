This is a simple web-based invoice generator created as a personal project to help musicians, particularly freelancers in Vienna and Austria, easily create professional invoices.

Usage Instructions (Click to Expand)

  • Fill in the fields on the form, including Your Name, Address, Orchestra info, Date of Concert, Fee, etc.
  • Preview your invoice in real-time to see how it will look. On desktop, the preview is shown on the right side, and on mobile devices, it appears at the bottom of the screen (scroll down to see PDF preview!).
  • Click the "Save as PDF" button to download your invoice.

Data Privacy and Security Info (Click to Expand)

All information you input into the invoice generator is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means that none of your data, including your name, address, account number, or any other personal information, is sent to any server.

The last entered data will be saved in your browser, so you don't need to re-enter everything each time you use the app. Please note that if you clear your browser's cache or localStorage, all saved data will be lost, and you will need to re-enter the information.
