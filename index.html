Invoice Generator for Musicians 🎻

This is a simple web-based invoice generator created as a personal project to help musicians, particularly freelancers in Vienna and Austria, easily create professional invoices.

Usage Instructions (Click to Expand)

  • Fill in the form, including your name, date of concert, fee, etc.
  • Preview your PDF invoice in real-time to see how it will look. (scroll down to see PDF preview!)
  • Click "Save as PDF" to download your invoice.

If you have any questions or encounter any issues, feel free to contact the developer at kateryna.jeeva@gmail.com. I'll be happy to help! 😊

Data Privacy and Security Info (Click to Expand)

  • None of your data is sent to any server!
  • All information you input here is stored locally in your browser.
  • No need to retype! (Unless you clear the cache)

Happy invoicing! 😊
